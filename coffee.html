Coffeeshop Recommendations

I like coffee, especially cappuccinos. My friend Justine has a fantastic list of good coffeeshops in a few places around the world. I won't quibble with her recommendations except to append a South Bay recommendation for Chromatic.

Note: When it comes to coffee, I only evaluate cappuccinos.

Boston/Cambridge

  • George Howell Coffee is the best Boston cappuccino I've had thus far. The flavor is well balanced, and the baristas pour excellently. This place is on the same level as Blue Bottle or Four Barrell in SF.
  • 1369 Coffee and Crema Cafe are good options in Cambridge. Crema uses George Howell beans and is also a good lunch spot.
  • Area Four has the best cappuccino near the Stata Center.
  • Flat Black, near Downtown, is operated by Australians and thus has flat whites; Pavement is a good option if you're in Back Bay.
  • Tealuxe is good for tea. Sadly (much like the rest of the East Coast) their boba is terrible, but they make a decent masala chai. They also have an exhaustive loose-leaf selection.
