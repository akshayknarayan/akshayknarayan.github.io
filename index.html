Akshay Narayan

I am a graduate student at MIT working with Hari Balakrishnan and Mohammad Alizadeh. I am supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. I previously received a Irwin Mark Jacobs and Joan Klein Jacobs Presidential Fellowship.

Prior to MIT I was a research assistant in the UC Berkeley NetSys lab working with Sylvia Ratnasamy and Scott Shenker. Before that I was an undergrad at UC Berkeley, from which I graduated with High Honors in 2015.

These are links to my CV, GitHub page, SSH Public Key, and LinkedIn profile. If you want to contact me, you can do so via Keybase, Twitter, or Email.
